# Fatal Flaws for Education Informatization Governance Ideas

Run this before scoring. A CRITICAL flaw short-circuits the evaluation and returns `Pivot` unless the user can repair it with concrete information.

| Flaw | Severity | Detection question | Repair strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Technology-first without governance problem | CRITICAL | Can the idea name a school, regional or institutional governance pain point without naming the tool first? | Reframe from stakeholder pain point, then decide whether the technology is necessary. |
| Ethics impossible | CRITICAL | Does the study require identifiable student/teacher data, opaque profiling or automated decisions without consent? | Redesign with data minimization, anonymization, human review and institutional approval. |
| Evidence mismatch | CRITICAL | Does the idea use model accuracy, response time or usage count to prove educational value or governance effectiveness? | Add organizational, behavioral, learning or stakeholder evidence. |
| Theory vacuum | MAJOR/CRITICAL | Is the framework or indicator system only a list of functions? | Select a theory and derive design principles from it. |
| Human role erased | MAJOR | Are teachers, administrators or students only data providers? | Add human interpretation, negotiation, feedback and override points. |
| Policy sloganization | MAJOR | Are policy terms used without operational implications? | Translate policy into measurable governance requirements. |
| Scope explosion | MAJOR | Does one study promise platform, mechanism, empirical proof and policy reform all at once? | Split into design, validation and implementation studies. |
| Data access fantasy | MAJOR | Does the idea assume clean cross-department data that the institution does not have? | Add data governance and staged pilot plan. |
| Equity blind spot | MAJOR | Could the system amplify differences across teachers, majors, regions or student groups? | Add fairness analysis and subgroup interpretation. |
| Tool dependency without durability | MINOR/MAJOR | Is the contribution tied to a product or model version? | Elevate to mechanism, architecture or governance principle. |
