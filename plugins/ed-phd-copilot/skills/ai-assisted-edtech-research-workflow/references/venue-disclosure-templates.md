# Venue Disclosure Templates

Use these templates when AI materially assists education technology or education informatization governance research. Always adapt the wording to the target journal, university, ethics committee or funder policy.

## Shared Rules

- Do not list AI tools as authors.
- State the task scope of AI assistance.
- State that the human researcher verified sources, evidence, analysis and final wording.
- Do not disclose confidential student, teacher, school, peer-review or unpublished manuscript data to unapproved external AI systems.
- Follow stricter venue rules when they differ from this template.

## Chinese CSSCI / Core Journal Submission

适用场景：中文期刊投稿，AI 参与提纲整理、语言润色、图示草案、文献矩阵或一致性检查。

```text
作者在论文准备过程中使用 AI 工具辅助进行提纲梳理、语言润色和结构一致性检查。AI 工具未参与研究选题、理论判断、数据解释和最终结论形成。作者已对文献来源、数据处理、分析过程和全文表述进行人工核验，并对论文内容承担全部学术责任。
```

If AI assisted code or data processing, add:

```text
涉及数据处理或分析代码的 AI 辅助部分均由作者复核，并在可复现材料中保留人工检查记录。
```

## Doctoral Proposal or Dissertation Defense

适用场景：开题报告、中期考核、预答辩、正式答辩或学位论文说明。

```text
本人在论文研究过程中使用 AI 工具辅助完成文献整理、写作提纲、语言修改和格式一致性检查。研究问题、理论框架、研究设计、数据解释、结论判断和最终文本均由本人负责。本人未将未脱敏的学生、教师、学校内部材料或涉密数据上传至未经学校批准的外部 AI 系统。
```

For dissertation appendices, add a brief AI-use log:

| Date/period | Tool/model if known | Task | Human verification |
|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM | Tool name | Outline/language/checklist/code assistance | Sources, data, claims and final wording verified by researcher |

## International Journal Submission

Use this when the journal does not provide exact wording.

```text
During manuscript preparation, AI tools were used to assist with outline organization, language revision, figure-label brainstorming and checklist-based consistency review. The author(s) verified all citations, data handling, analysis decisions, interpretations and final claims, and remain fully responsible for the manuscript.
```

If the journal asks for method-level disclosure, add:

```text
No AI tool was used to generate research data, replace human coding judgment, make participant-level decisions or determine the study conclusions.
```

## Ethics Review / IRB Application

适用场景：提交涉及学生、教师、课堂、平台日志或学校治理材料的伦理审查。

```text
本研究可能使用 AI 工具辅助访谈提纲优化、文本初步整理、代码脚本检查和报告语言润色。AI 工具不会接触可识别个人身份的数据，不会替代研究者进行编码判断、风险判断或结论解释。所有进入 AI 工具的材料均将进行去标识化处理，并遵守学校数据安全、知情同意和访问控制要求。
```

## Red-Line Addendum

If any of the following occurred, do not use a normal disclosure template. Stop and repair the workflow first:

- identifiable student or teacher data were uploaded to an unapproved external AI tool;
- AI-generated citations were used without source verification;
- AI output replaced human coding, interpretation or participant-level decisions;
- peer-review or unpublished confidential material was uploaded without permission;
- the manuscript hides material AI use where the venue requires disclosure.
