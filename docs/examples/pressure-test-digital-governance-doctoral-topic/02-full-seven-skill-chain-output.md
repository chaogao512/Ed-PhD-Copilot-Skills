## Main Output

### 1. Idea Evaluation Result

**Verdict:**可开题，可扩展为博士论文，但必须将题目从“数字化转型效果”收束为“数据治理能力建设机制”。

**Topic rewrite:**

> 县域义务教育数字化转型中的教育数据治理能力建设机制研究。

**Next Skill:** `governance-paper-template`.

### 2. Paper Template Result

**Manuscript type:**治理机制型 + 案例研究型 + 博士论文拓展型。

**Doctoral expansion path:**

| Chapter group | Function | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Chapter 1 | Problem, policy background and research design | verified policy sources, field problem statement |
| Chapter 2 | Literature and theory | verified Chinese and international literature; unverified candidates excluded from formal references |
| Chapter 3 | Analytical framework | organizational capacity, data governance, education leadership |
| Chapter 4 | County case and governance context | county documents, platform structure, school profiles |
| Chapter 5 | Responsibility and process mechanism | interviews, process documents, actor-responsibility matrix |
| Chapter 6 | Evidence of capacity construction | survey, logs, cross-case comparison, governance artifacts |
| Chapter 7 | Risk control and improvement path | risk register, ethics audit, policy/practice recommendations |

**Evidence Design Needed:** separate governance-process evidence from education-outcome evidence.

### 3. Mixed-Methods Evidence Result

**Audit Depth:** Strict.

| Claim type | Minimum evidence | Allowed wording |
|---|---|---|
| Capacity framework | policy text, expert consultation, case documents | "提出县域教育数据治理能力框架" |
| Mechanism explanation | COREQ-style interviews, coding records, responsibility matrix | "解释能力建设的组织机制" |
| School difference | survey, logs, cross-case comparison | "揭示不同学校的数据治理实践差异" |
| Risk control | ethics records, access logs, incident/complaint route | "识别风险并提出防控机制" |
| Learning outcome impact | longitudinal or quasi-experimental evidence | not supported in the baseline design |

**Claim downgrades:** strong effectiveness claims must become mechanism or process claims unless additional longitudinal/experimental evidence exists.

### 4. Introduction Result

**Four-part logic:**

| Part | Draft logic |
|---|---|
| Policy demand | 教育数字化要求县域治理从资源配置走向数据支持的质量改进。 |
| Technology affordance | 区域平台、学习数据和质量监测工具使跨校协同和精准支持成为可能。 |
| Alienation contradiction | 数据汇聚并不自动形成治理能力，现实中仍存在权责不清、数据质量不稳、教师负担转移和学生数据权益风险。 |
| Research legitimacy | 因此，需要研究县域教育数据治理能力如何形成、如何运行、如何被证据检验以及如何被伦理和制度边界约束。 |

**V1.5 Evidence and Risk Boundary:**引言不得声称“系统综述表明”除非 PRISMA-style 检索、筛选和纳入记录完整；不得把平台数据接入量写成治理能力本身。

### 5. Figure Design Result

**Figure type:** doctoral proposal mechanism figure + evidence chain figure.

**Recommended artifacts:**

- Main mechanism figure: data subjects -> data sources -> governance capacity layer -> educational use -> oversight and accountability.
- Research design figure: policy/text evidence, interview evidence, survey/log evidence and ethics evidence converge into a claim-evidence matrix.

**Quality requirement:** high-risk student-related data must show notice, authorization, minimization, human review, appeal/correction, retention/deletion and audit trail.

### 6. Pre-Submission Review Result

**Review Depth:** Strict.

| Severity | Finding | Required repair |
|---|---|---|
| CRITICAL if present | identifiable student or teacher data used in AI tools or shared without authorization | add data-management plan, de-identification and external-AI non-upload rule |
| MAJOR | topic slides into platform-performance evaluation | restore governance capacity as the research object in title, RQs and findings |
| MAJOR | causal conclusion without comparison or longitudinal design | rewrite as mechanism explanation or association |
| MAJOR | interview method lacks COREQ transparency | add sampling, role, protocol, coding and quotation evidence |
| MINOR | policy prose is slogan-like | translate policy into governance requirements and observable routines |

### 7. AI-Assisted Workflow Result

**Human-AI boundary:** AI may help organize literature matrices, draft interview prompts, produce figure sketches, check claim-evidence consistency and polish language. The researcher must own research questions, theory selection, ethics approval, data interpretation, citation verification and final claims.

**Disclosure route:** use `venue-disclosure-templates.md`.

| Venue | Disclosure action |
|---|---|
| Doctoral proposal/defense | add an AI-use statement and simple AI-use log |
| Chinese journal | disclose material AI assistance in outline, language, figure or consistency review |
| Ethics review | state that AI will not receive identifiable student/teacher/school data |
| International journal | adapt target journal's AI policy and state human verification |

## Quality Self-Check

- The seven-skill chain preserved the same research object: county education data governance capacity.
- No step converted governance capacity into platform performance.
- Evidence boundaries were consistent across method design, introduction and review.
- Figure and AI workflow rules made high-risk education data use visible.
- Chinese CSSCI/core-journal sources were not fabricated or promoted without verification.

## Remaining Gaps

- Full doctoral use still requires verified Chinese journal literature through CNKI, journal websites, DOI pages, local PDFs or school database export.
- Real installed-skill trigger testing remains deferred until the user explicitly approves installation.
- If the dissertation later claims student-learning impact, an additional longitudinal or quasi-experimental chapter design is required.
