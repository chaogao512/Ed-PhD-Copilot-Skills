# Failure Case 10: Undisclosed AI Use and Sensitive Data Upload

## Weak input

“我把学生访谈原文、课堂视频转写和未发表论文都发给在线 AI，让它帮我编码和润色。投稿时不要提 AI 使用，免得编辑觉得不严谨。”

## Expected skill response

Use `ai-assisted-edtech-research-workflow` and `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer`.

## Expected diagnosis

- **CRITICAL: Confidentiality and data-protection risk**. Student interviews and classroom video transcripts may contain identifiable or sensitive education data.
- **CRITICAL: Disclosure integrity risk**. Hiding material AI use can violate journal, university or funder policy.
- **CRITICAL: Human responsibility gap**. AI-assisted coding cannot replace researcher interpretation, audit trail and ethics accountability.
- **MAJOR: Manuscript confidentiality risk**. Uploading an unpublished manuscript to an unapproved external AI system may breach institutional or publisher expectations.

## Required repair

Stop using the external AI workflow until data governance is reviewed. Then:

1. remove identifiable personal and institutional information;
2. check consent, ethics approval, platform data policy and university rules;
3. rebuild qualitative coding with researcher audit trail and human interpretation;
4. document AI tool, task scope and verification;
5. prepare an accurate disclosure note for the target venue;
6. state that AI is not an author and does not bear responsibility.
