# Pressure Test: Doctoral Topic Chain

> Version: V1.6
> Purpose: test whether the core skill chain can handle an authentic education informatization governance doctoral topic without drifting into a generic technology-development proposal.

## Topic

县域义务教育数字化转型中的教育数据治理能力建设研究。

## Scenario Boundary

This pressure test assumes a doctoral student in educational leadership and management wants to study how a county-level education bureau and its schools build data governance capacity during education digitalization.

The topic is intentionally difficult because it combines policy translation, organizational coordination, platform data, teacher workload, student privacy, school improvement and evidence boundaries. A weak skill chain would turn it into a platform construction plan or a dashboard evaluation. A mature skill chain should keep the governance problem visible.

## Chain Tested

1. `governance-idea-evaluator`
2. `governance-paper-template`
3. `mixed-methods-evidence-template`
4. `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer`

## Evidence Anchors Used

- Chinese official policy and standard sources already verified in `docs/chinese-core-literature-verified.md`
- NIST AI RMF, OECD AI Principles, EU AI Act and UNESCO AI guidance for AI/data governance boundaries
- PRISMA, COREQ, CONSORT/SPIRIT, MMAT and WWC-style evidence boundaries for method transparency
- Model Cards and Datasheets for Datasets for system/data documentation patterns

## Expected Pass Condition

The chain passes only if:

- the research object is governance capacity, not platform performance;
- data governance responsibilities and affected groups are explicit;
- causal and effectiveness claims are downgraded unless the design can support them;
- AI/data use includes privacy, fairness, appeal, oversight and monitoring;
- every next-step output preserves the same research problem rather than silently changing the topic.
