# Doctoral Stage Templates

Use these templates when turning an education informatization governance topic into doctoral-stage outputs. Keep the core research object stable across all stages.

## Shared Doctoral Quality Gates

- The topic must name the governance object, affected actors and education setting.
- Theoretical concepts must produce analytical dimensions, not only decorate the introduction.
- Method design must state what each evidence source can and cannot support.
- Ethics and data governance must appear before data collection, not after findings.
- Chinese CSSCI/core-journal records must remain candidates until verified through CNKI, journal site, DOI page, local PDF or school database export.

## Opening Proposal Template

Use for 开题报告.

1. Title and topic narrowing.
2. Policy background and practical problem.
3. Literature map and research gap.
4. Theoretical framework and core concepts.
5. Research questions.
6. Analytical framework or mechanism model.
7. Data sources and method design.
8. Ethics, data governance and AI-use disclosure.
9. Expected contribution and feasibility.
10. Work plan and risk control.

## Mid-Term Review Template

Use for 中期考核.

1. Original research question and any justified narrowing.
2. Completed literature verification and remaining candidate sources.
3. Completed fieldwork, data collection or artifact construction.
4. Early findings and evidence strength.
5. Method deviations and repair plan.
6. Ethics/data-governance compliance record.
7. Chapter completion status.
8. Next-stage schedule.

## Pre-Defense Template

Use for 预答辩.

1. Dissertation argument in one paragraph.
2. Chapter-by-chapter contribution check.
3. Claim-evidence matrix.
4. Theory-method-fit audit.
5. Figure/table audit.
6. AI-use and citation-integrity audit.
7. Known limitations and defensible wording.
8. 30-day revision plan.

## Final Defense Template

Use for 正式答辩.

1. Research problem and significance.
2. Theoretical framework.
3. Method and data legitimacy.
4. Main findings.
5. Innovation and contribution.
6. Practical implications for education governance.
7. Ethics, privacy and responsible AI/data-use statement.
8. Limitations and future research.

## Dissertation Claim-Evidence Matrix

| Claim | Evidence | Boundary | Defense wording |
|---|---|---|---|
| Framework contribution | policy, literature, expert review | context-bound unless broadly validated | "构建并初步验证..." |
| Mechanism explanation | interviews, case documents, process tracing | explains selected cases | "揭示...机制" |
| Practice difference | survey, logs, cross-case comparison | association, not causation | "呈现...差异" |
| Effectiveness | requires stronger comparison or longitudinal design | not supported by descriptive evidence | "提供后续验证基础" |
