# Section Skeleton for Governance System and Mechanism Papers

## Recommended structure

1. **Introduction**: policy demand, technology possibility, governance contradiction, contribution.
2. **Problem context and theoretical foundation**: scenario, stakeholders, theory anchors and research questions.
3. **Design principles**: derive principles from theory and governance requirements.
4. **Architecture or mechanism**: present the dual topology, actor roles, data governance and human oversight.
5. **Validation or evidence design**: expert consultation, DSR evaluation, case study or mixed evidence.
6. **Discussion**: theoretical contribution, practical pathways, ethics and limitations.
7. **Conclusion**: concise claims bounded by evidence.

## Contribution check

- Theoretical contribution: What concept, framework or mechanism is advanced?
- Method/design contribution: What design principles or artifact are reusable?
- Practical contribution: What implementation pathway is offered?
- Policy implication: What governance implication follows, without overclaiming?
