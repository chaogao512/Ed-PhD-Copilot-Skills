# Education Informatization Governance Paper Types

| Type | Core contribution | Typical method | Main risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Governance framework paper | Conceptual model or governance framework | Theory synthesis, policy analysis, expert consultation | Empty framework without mechanism |
| Smart campus architecture paper | Socio-technical layered architecture | Design science, case demonstration, expert review | Engineering blueprint without education logic |
| Human-AI collaboration mechanism paper | Actor roles, feedback and accountability mechanism | Mechanism modeling, scenario analysis, interviews | Human-in-the-loop only as slogan |
| Design science research artifact paper | Artifact plus design principles and evaluation | DSR build-evaluate cycle | Artifact not evaluated rigorously |
| Indicator system paper | Theory-derived and validated indicators | Literature coding, Delphi, AHP/EFA/CFA | Indicator list lacks construct validity |
| Organizational change paper | Implementation pathway and capacity building | Case study, mixed methods, longitudinal evidence | Technology adoption treated as simple training |
| Teacher digital competence governance paper | Organizational support mechanism for teacher capacity building | Mixed methods, case study, competence rubric, classroom artifact analysis | Treating competence as training attendance |
| Education data governance paper | Data rights, standards, quality, risk and accountability mechanism | Policy analysis, process audit, interviews, expert consultation | Treating a data platform as governance |
| Human-AI assessment governance paper | Formative evaluation mechanism with AI support and human responsibility | Design science, classroom observation, log analysis, interviews | Treating AI scoring accuracy as evaluation quality |

## Routing

- Architecture or mechanism -> use `dual-topology.md` and `section-skeleton.md`.
- Indicator/evaluation -> route to `mixed-methods-evidence-template`.
- Early idea -> route back to `governance-idea-evaluator`.

## Paper-Type Selection Rules

- If the central object is teacher capacity, choose teacher digital competence governance rather than generic training design.
- If the central object is data sharing, choose education data governance rather than smart campus architecture unless a technical artifact is the main contribution.
- If the central object is classroom feedback, choose human-AI assessment governance and require formative assessment logic.
- If the central object is a platform or Agent system, choose smart campus architecture only after naming actors, responsibility and evidence boundaries.
- If the contribution is an artifact, add design science reporting requirements and do not stop at conceptual framework.
