# Style Anti-Patterns

| Anti-pattern | Why it weakens the paper | Repair |
|---|---|---|
| AI-flavored praise | Sounds generic and unverifiable | Replace with mechanism and evidence |
| Technology worship | Ignores organization and human agency | State conditions and constraints |
| “Empowerment” without mechanism | Conceptual inflation | Name actor, capability, process and evidence |
| Engineering jargon | Alienates education reviewers | Translate into educational function |
| Conclusion outruns evidence | Invites reviewer rejection | Narrow claim to measured evidence |
| Policy sloganization | Weak problem framing | Explain institutional contradiction |
