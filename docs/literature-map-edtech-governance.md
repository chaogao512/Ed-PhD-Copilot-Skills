## 2. Research Themes for Skill Adaptation

### 2.1 Education digitalization and governance

Core pattern:

- Avoid equating platform construction with governance modernization.
- Examine whether data, actors, rules and responsibilities are reorganized.
- Treat digitalization as a socio-technical transformation, not an IT procurement project.

Skill use:

- `governance-idea-evaluator`: score organizational feasibility and governance problem clarity.
- `governance-paper-template`: require technology-organization dual topology.
- `governance-figure-designer`: show policy, data governance, actors and accountability.

### 2.2 Human-centred learning analytics and AIED

Searched material on human-centred LA/AIED highlights privacy, agency, stakeholder involvement, trustworthiness and the balance between automation and human control.

Skill use:

- Add end-user involvement to design and evaluation.
- Treat student and teacher agency as evidence dimensions.
- Require explanation, override and appeal routes for high-stakes recommendations.

### 2.3 LLMs and practical/ethical challenges in education

LLM education reviews identify use cases such as feedback, grading, recommendation, profiling and teaching support, while warning about transparency, replicability, privacy and beneficence.

Skill use:

- `ai-assisted-edtech-research-workflow` must reject fabricated citations, data and results.
- `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer` must flag opaque profiling and hidden high-stakes decisions.
- `mixed-methods-evidence-template` must distinguish technical feasibility from educational value.

### 2.4 Teacher digital competence and AI literacy

Teacher-facing AI competency frameworks emphasize human-centred mindset, ethics, AI foundations, pedagogy and professional learning.

Skill use:

- Teacher digital competence studies should not rely on platform logs alone.
- Evidence should include self-report, performance artifacts, interviews and classroom-use context.
- Governance designs should include teacher workload and professional-development pathways.

### 2.5 Smart campus and education data governance

Smart campus research should be reframed from “data collection and dashboard display” to “data standards, authorization, quality, accountability and improvement loop”.

Skill use:

- `governance-paper-template`: require data governance layer.
- `governance-figure-designer`: show data lifecycle and responsibility.
- `edtech-pre-submission-reviewer`: flag “data island” and “dashboard determinism” problems.

## 3. Method Sources and Skill Translation

| Method source | URL | Translation into skill rules |
|---|---|---|
| Design science research guidelines | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Design_science_(methodology) | Require artifact, problem relevance, rigorous evaluation, contribution and communication to both technical and management audiences |
| Delphi method | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delphi_method | Require expert selection, multiple rounds, anonymized feedback, predefined stopping/consensus criteria and reporting of revision logic |
| Mixed methods / multimethodology | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multimethodology | Require integration, not parallel listing; use triangulation matrices and claim-evidence boundaries |
| COREQ / qualitative reporting overview | https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EQUATOR_Network | Require transparent reporting for interviews, focus groups and qualitative trustworthiness |
